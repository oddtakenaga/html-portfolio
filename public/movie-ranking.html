The Best Movies According to Audrey

My top 3 movies of all time.

Coraline

This is a childhood favorite of mine, and I know almost all the lines by heart. The amazing voice acting, peculiar and slightly spooky story line, and the stop motion all culminate into a great, unforgettable film.

Spirited Away

I recently watched this in Japanese, and it honestly was such a heartwarming and beautiful movie. The culture was evident through it.

The Martian

In addition to the charismatic Matt Damon, the story of the Martian includes tension and keeps you glued to your seat while entertained.
